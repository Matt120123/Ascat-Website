About Asic


Asia Source iCollege was established on November 27, 2013 under the leadership of Mr. Fernando G. Dulce. The school is on its present location at the 4th Floor of Gate 3 Plaza, along Lawton Avenue corner Juliano Ave in AFPOVAI Phase III, Western Bicutan, Taguig City. The School's guiding principle is communicated in its motto, “Your Success is our Goal.”


“Your Success is our Goal.” has been the mantra of Asia Source iCollege aspiring to be the Asia’s best in the fields of arts and technology.


A relatively new school, Asia Source is abreast with other top institutions and remarkably exceeds expectations of a new school in providing quality education relevant with the needs of the times through its top of the line facilities, highly-qualified educators, and government-approved instructional content validated by the seal of recognition and certification by both the Department of Education (DepEd) and Technical Education and Skills Development Authority (TESDA). The school offers Short Courses, Assessment and Scholarship programs under TESDA since 2014 while Senior High School programs started on June 2016 under Department of Education.


A second branch was opened at Kapasigan, Pasig City on January 23, 2018 offering the same courses we have in Taguig City.


Asia Source iCollege offers a very low investment to help the students finish a technical –vocational and senior-high school programs with ease but promises a great and successful future ahead of them.


Success comes to those who become success conscious, who are busy looking for it and those who dare to dream. We at Asia Source’s goal is to make you become successful in life not just by preparing for job opportunities but also an entrepreneur through partnership with top-rank companies.

Vision


Asia Source envisions to be the premier institution that produces morally upright leaders with technological and entreprenuerial expertise and is actively responsive to the challenges and the needs of the global market. It values the presence of its workforce, thus, it provides equal opportunities for total growth and development through dynamic and strategic leadership, enhancing the skills and abilities to become world class business partners.

Mission


Asia Source iCollege has an ardent commitment for excellence. It provides a high quality education with an innovative way of thinking through its work of the art and dynamic facilties. This educational institution is the avenue where character is indoctrinated to its stakeholders to become value-oriented leaders with emphasis on integrity, discipline, compassion, and respect. Thus, emanate leaders who are well-rounded with a sense of global success through innovation, collaboration, and a spirit of passion and determination in every endeavor they traverse.

Core Values

SELF-DISCIPLINE (Disiplina sa sarili)

  • It is the manifestation to forego instant fulfilment in goodwill of something better, but rather is the aptitude of the person to fuse to actions, thoughts and behaviour, which lead to success, in spite of challenges and adversities of life.

INTEGRITY (Pagkamatuwid)

  • It is the moral compass of doing the right actions that would do nothing to dishonour your true self.

RESPECT (Paggalang)

  • It is the high esteem and care of showing value to another being and nature. It is the unspoken communication which builds strong relations towards people, world and nature.

COMPASSION (Malasakit)

  • It is to recognize the suffering of others and take action to help and touching the heart of a man regardless of the stature of one’s life.

SERVICE (Paglilingkod)

  • It is the diligence and selflessly giving time and effort in fulfilling the needs of another individual and see it through to completion.

EXCELLENCE (Kahusayan)

  • We surpass mediocrity and believes in the fundamental purpose of quality education through innovation and continuous development of curricula, instruction material, facilities and equipment and the major key source of information of the students- the professional character and expertise of the institution’s officials, instructors and staff to produce distinct and exceptional graduates.
