CONGRATULATIONS TO THE OVERALL CHAMPION OF ASIA SOURCE

iCOLLEGE SPORTS FESTIVAL 2023 - 2024

June 23, 2024 • Press Patriots Journalism Club

Asia Source iCollege Sports Festival has just ended, and we are excited to announce the overall champion: Team Hippogriff. They won many gold medals, which helped them secure this top spot.

Team Hippogriff showed amazing skills, teamwork, and sportsmanship throughout the festival. They performed well in many different events, proving their hard work and dedication. Each team member played a key role in their success. Winning so many gold medals across various sports shows just how talented Team Hippogriff is. Whether it was running, jumping, or team games, they stood out and achieved great results.

House Hippogriff from Taguig Branch, overall champion of the Asia Source iCollege Sports Fest 2023-2024. Photo taken by the Pixels Pixies Multimedia Club of Pasig branch.

The festival saw many teams and athletes competing fiercely, making Team Hippogriff's victories even more impressive. Their consistent performance and smart strategies helped them rise to the top. Their journey was full of inspiring moments, teamwork, and outstanding athleticism.

We congratulate Team Hippogriff for their fantastic success. They have set a new standard for future festivals and have truly earned their moment in the spotlight. Their victory should inspire future participants to aim high and show great sportsmanship.

Once again, congratulations to Team Hippogriff, the overall champion of the Asia Source iCollege Sports Festival!
